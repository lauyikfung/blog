Japan Mahjong

这是一个基于C++的带有图形用户界面的单机游戏程序。其包含了大部分日本麻将常见役种，例如一杯口、岭上开花等等，并用实际规则计算点数。玩家与3名电脑AI对战，AI策略为人工设计的基于牌型分数的决策。合作者为刘益枫、张焯扬、庞常毓、仲柯源、李子麒。
This is a offline game program with GUI based on C++. It includes most of the common service species of Japanese mahjong, such as ipeko, linshankaiho, etc., and the points is calculated according to the actual rules. The player plays against three AI, whose strategy is a manually designed decision based on the card type score. The collaborators are Liu Yifeng, Zhang Zhuoyang, Pang Changyu, Zhong Keyuan and Li Ziqi.
